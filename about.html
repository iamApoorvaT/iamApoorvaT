A little bit about me

Hi! I am a student from India who has been studying psychology since the year 2018. I originally started off as a science student, completed my high school education in it, and then moved on to follow my passion to pursue psychology.

I completed my bachelor's degree in Psychology from a college affiliated with the University of Mumbai and completed my M.A. in Psychology (specialization: Social Psychology) from the Department of Applied Psychology at the University of Mumbai. While studying for these two programs, I gained a lot of knowledge of different research methodologies and experience through my coursework and projects.

A mix-method researcher who is open to learning about more research methodologies. I am preparing to apply for a PhD in Social Psychology to further pursue my research interests.

As of the month of February, I started working as a Research Affiliate at Monk Prayogshala, other than that I am also a course mentor for the Social Psychology Course by Weslyean University on Coursera.

Then in the month of April, I started working as an External Research Asistant at Identity and Intergroup Relations Lab @ Ashoka University under the guidance of Dr. Sramana Majumdar.

An avid reader, I enjoy reading along with a cup of coffee or herbal tea in my free time. I have a passion for travel. I'm always open to new experiences, cultures, and ways of thinking. Other than that I also love Asian language media such as anime, manga (comics), light novels and dramas. I also like to paint!
